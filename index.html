About the project

The Mastercraft Bamboo Monitor Riser is a sturdy and stylish platform that elevates your screen to a more comfortable viewing height. Placing your monitor at eye level has the portential to improve your posture and make you more comfortable while at work, helping you stay focus on the task at hand

Featuring artisan craftsmanship, the simplicity of design creates extra desk space below your computer to allow notepads, pens, USB sticks to be stored under the stand
